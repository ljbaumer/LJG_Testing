# Prompt: Weekly Rollup

Analyze the provided notes created or modified in the last 7 days and synthesize a high-level weekly summary.

## Rules
- Focus on connections between notes.
- Identify themes and momentum.
- Keep sections concise.

## Required Sections
1. **Wins**: Progress made, notes completed, or projects moved forward.
2. **Open Questions**: Unresolved issues, missing information, or new curiosities identified in the notes.
3. **Next Steps**: Higher-level priorities for the coming week based on new information.
4. **Key Source Links**: A list of the most significant notes from this week with a one-sentence explanation of why they matter.

## Output Format
- Clean Markdown.
